## Brainstorming Session Overview

Brainstorming provides a free and open environment that encourages everyone within a team to participate in the creative thinking process that leads to problem solving. Prioritizing volume over value, out-of-the-box ideas are welcome and built upon, and all participants are encouraged to collaborate, helping each other develop a rich amount of creative solutions.

## Step-1: Team Gathering, Collaboration and Select the Problem Statement

### Before You Collaborate

A little bit of preparation goes a long way with this session. Here's what you need to do to get going:

#### 1.1 Define Your Problem Statement

**What problem are you trying to solve?** Frame your problem as a How Might We statement. This will be the focus of your brainstorm.

**How Might We** help users find personalized inspirational quotes through conversational AI?

**Time:** 10 minutes to prepare | 1 hour to collaborate | 2-8 people recommended

#### 1.2 Team Gathering

Define who should participate in the session and send an invite. Share relevant information or pre-work ahead.

#### 1.3 Set the Goal

Think about the problem you'll be focusing on solving in the brainstorming session.

**Goal:** Create an intelligent chatbot that recommends quotes based on user emotions and preferences using Natural Language Processing.

#### 1.4 Learn How to Use the Facilitation Tools

Use the Facilitation Superpowers to run a happy and productive session.

---

## Key Rules of Brainstorming

To run a smooth and productive session:

- **Stay in topic** - Encourage wild ideas
- **Defer judgment** - Listen to others
- **Go for volume** - If possible, be visual
- **Build on ideas** - Use yes, and... approach
